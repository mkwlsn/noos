# fills

The paints used to fill the area of the shape. For help on how to change this value, see [Editing Properties](/plugin-docs/editing-properties/).

 Supported on:

- BooleanOperationNode
- ComponentNode
- ComponentSetNode
- EllipseNode
- FrameNode
- HighlightNode
- InstanceNode
- LineNode
- PolygonNode
- RectangleNode
- SectionNode
- ShapeWithTextNode
- SlideNode
- StampNode
- StarNode
- StickyNode
- TableCellNode
- TableNode
- TextNode
- TextPathNode
- TextSublayerNode
