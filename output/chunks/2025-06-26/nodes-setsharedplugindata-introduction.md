# setSharedPluginData

Lets you store custom information on any node or style, public to all plugins. The total size of your entry (`namespace`, `key`, `value`) cannot exceed 100 kB.

 Supported on:

- BooleanOperationNode
- CodeBlockNode
- ComponentNode
- ComponentSetNode
- ConnectorNode
- DocumentNode
- EffectStyle
- EllipseNode
- EmbedNode
- FrameNode
- GridStyle
- GroupNode
- HighlightNode
- InstanceNode

- LineNode
- LinkUnfurlNode
- MediaNode
- PageNode
- PaintStyle

- SlideNode
