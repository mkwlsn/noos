# setPluginData

Lets you store custom information on any node or style, private to your plugin. The total size of your entry (`pluginId`, `key`, `value`) cannot exceed 100 kB.

 Supported on:

- BooleanOperationNode
- CodeBlockNode
- ComponentNode
- ComponentSetNode
- ConnectorNode
- DocumentNode
- EffectStyle
- EllipseNode
- EmbedNode
- FrameNode
- GridStyle
- GroupNode
- HighlightNode
- InstanceNode
- InteractiveSlideElementNode
- LineNode
- LinkUnfurlNode
- MediaNode
- PageNode
- PaintStyle
- PolygonNode
- RectangleNode
- SectionNode
- ShapeWithTextNode
- SliceNode
- SlideGridNode
- SlideNode
- SlideRowNode
- StampNode
- StarNode
- StickyNode
- TableNode
- TextNode
- TextPathNode
- TextStyle
- TransformGroupNode
- Variable
- VariableCollection
- VectorNode
- WashiTapeNode
- WidgetNode
