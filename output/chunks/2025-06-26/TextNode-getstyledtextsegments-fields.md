An array of text properties. Any text property that can apply to specific character ranges is supported:

- fontSize
- fontName
- fontWeight
- textDecoration
- textDecorationStyle
- textDecorationOffset
- textDecorationThickness
- textDecorationColor
- textDecorationSkipInk
- textCase
- lineHeight
- letterSpacing
- fills
- textStyleId
- fillStyleId
- listOptions
- listSpacing
- indentation
- paragraphIndent
- paragraphSpacing
- hyperlink
- boundVariables
- textStyleOverrides
- openTypeFeatures
