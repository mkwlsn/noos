# stuckNodes

An array of nodes that are "stuck" to this node. In FigJam, stamps, highlights, and some widgets can "stick"
to other nodes if they are dragged on top of another node.

 Supported on:

- BooleanOperationNode
- CodeBlockNode
- ComponentNode
- ComponentSetNode
- ConnectorNode
- EllipseNode
- EmbedNode
- FrameNode
- GroupNode
- HighlightNode
- InstanceNode
- InteractiveSlideElementNode
- LineNode
- LinkUnfurlNode
- MediaNode
- PolygonNode
- RectangleNode
- SectionNode
- ShapeWithTextNode
- SliceNode
- SlideGridNode
- SlideNode
- SlideRowNode
- StampNode
- StarNode
- StickyNode
- TableNode
- TextNode
- TextPathNode
- TransformGroupNode
- VectorNode
- WashiTapeNode
- WidgetNode
