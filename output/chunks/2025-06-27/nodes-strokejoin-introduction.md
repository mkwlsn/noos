# strokeJoin

The decoration applied to vertices which have two or more connected segments.

 Supported on:

- BooleanOperationNode
- ComponentNode
- ComponentSetNode
- ConnectorNode
- EllipseNode
- FrameNode
- HighlightNode
- InstanceNode
- LineNode
- PolygonNode
- RectangleNode
- ShapeWithTextNode
- SlideNode
- StampNode
- StarNode
- TextNode
- TextPathNode
- VectorNode
- WashiTapeNode
